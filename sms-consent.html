SMS Consent & Opt-In Policy

Three Day Weekend, LLC

Last Updated: January 10, 2025

Overview

This page documents how Three Day Weekend, LLC collects consent from users who wish to receive SMS text messages. We are committed to transparency and compliance with all applicable SMS messaging regulations, including TCPA (Telephone Consumer Protection Act) and CTIA guidelines.

Important: SMS Consent is Optional

Opting in to receive SMS text messages from Three Day Weekend is completely optional and is NOT required to access or use any of our services. You can purchase, use, and receive full support for our automation and back-office solutions without ever providing consent to receive text messages.

How Consent is Collected

Important: Phone Number Collection vs. SMS Opt-In

Phone numbers are collected for essential service functions such as account management, customer support, and appointment scheduling. Opting in to receive promotional or informational SMS messages is a completely separate, optional choice that is NOT required for any service access.

1. Explicit Opt-In Process

Users who wish to receive SMS messages from Three Day Weekend must explicitly opt in through one of the following methods:

Express Written Consent - Separate from Service Registration

Users may provide their phone number for service purposes (account creation, customer support, consultation scheduling). SMS messaging consent is collected separately through an optional, unchecked checkbox that is distinct from phone number collection for service use.

  • Website Contact Form: Phone numbers may be collected for service purposes (support, scheduling). A separate, optional, unchecked checkbox allows users to opt in to SMS notifications. The checkbox is distinct from the phone number field and clearly labeled for SMS consent only.
  • Email Request: Users already using our services can email info@threeday.llc to specifically request SMS opt-in
  • Verbal/Written Agreement: Existing customers can opt in to SMS during phone calls or in-person meetings, which is documented in our CRM as a separate action from service registration

2. Example Consent Language

SMS opt-in is voluntary and separate from service access. Users are not required to consent to text messaging in order to use our services.

Sample Opt-In Form Text:

Phone Number: [__________________]
(Used for account access, customer support, and appointment scheduling)

Optional: Receive SMS Notifications
☐ I agree to receive text messages from Three Day Weekend, LLC at the phone number provided above. I understand that message and data rates may apply, and I can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Note: The SMS checkbox above must be unchecked by default and is visually separated from the phone number field. Opting in to SMS is completely optional and is not a condition of purchasing or using Three Day Weekend services. The phone number is collected for service purposes only.

3. Confirmation Message

Upon opting in, users receive an immediate confirmation SMS:

"Welcome to Three Day Weekend! You've successfully opted in to receive updates about our automation solutions for tree care companies. Reply STOP to opt out anytime. Msg&data rates may apply."

What Users Receive

Users who opt in may receive the following types of text messages:

  • Service notifications: System alerts, workflow status updates, automation completion confirmations
  • Appointment reminders: Scheduled consultation confirmations, meeting reminders, calendar notifications
  • Account updates: Important account-related information such as subscription changes, billing notifications, or service updates
  • Support responses: Replies to inquiries submitted via our website or email

Message Frequency: Varies based on user activity and requests. Typically no more than 4 messages per month.

Reminder: Opting in to SMS is not required to receive these services. All updates and notifications are also available through email and our web dashboard.

User Rights & Opt-Out

How to Opt Out

Users can opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  1. Replying STOP to any message from us
  2. Replying UNSUBSCRIBE to any message
  3. Emailing info@threeday.llc with the subject line "SMS Opt-Out"

Opt-Out Confirmation

Upon opting out, users receive an immediate confirmation:

"You've been unsubscribed from Three Day Weekend SMS messages. You won't receive further messages from us. Reply START to resubscribe."

Help Command

Users can reply HELP at any time to receive assistance:

"Three Day Weekend: For help, email info@threeday.llc or reply STOP to opt out. Msg&data rates may apply."

Data Protection & Privacy

  • Phone numbers are stored securely and never shared with third parties
  • We comply with all applicable privacy regulations
  • Users can request deletion of their data by emailing info@threeday.llc
  • All SMS communications are sent via compliant messaging platforms (Twilio)

Terms & Conditions

  • Consent is optional: SMS opt-in is NOT required to purchase, access, or use any Three Day Weekend services
  • Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan
  • Supported carriers include major US carriers (AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, etc.)
  • We are not responsible for delayed or undelivered messages
  • You can access all services and support without consenting to SMS communications

Compliance Statement

Three Day Weekend, LLC is committed to compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), CTIA Messaging Principles and Best Practices, and all applicable federal and state regulations governing SMS communications. We maintain records of all opt-ins and opt-outs as required by law.

Contact Information
